About Me:
I work in .Net enterprise software development. I can program proficiently in C# and have used C++, C, Python in multiple projects.
I am passionate about game development and have used Unity and Godot to create projects which you can see and play on my itch io page.
I study computer science and mathematics at QUT and have a 6.5 GPA. I have strong mathematics skills that complement my programming skills and enhance my approaches to problem solving.
I have a passion for music and use my musicianship and sound-design skills to create sound effects, compose soundtracks, produce, mix and master atmospheres and communicate feelings in my projects and collaboratively. I use Ableton Live and record my own playing of electric bass, guitar, keys and alto sax, and Serum and Vital as my primary synthesisers for sound design.

Experience:
- I'm in my penultimate year of my Computer Science & Mathematics degree At QUT, in which I completed numerous programming projects. My favourites were in IGB190 we created an ARPG in a group. In CAB202, microprocessors and digital systems, I completed an embedded systems project in C, creating a pattern memory game able to be played with a microcontroller with 4 buttons and a 7-segment display. In IGB102 I created a analogue synthesiser out of a raspberry pi. In CAB203 a discrete mathematics unit we used mathematical concepts to solve real world problems with python.
- I've completed multiple projects using C# and Unity, both at QUT and in my own time.

- Talking Dungeon : A turn based 2d pixel art RPG similar to the likes of Chrono Trigger and Undertale.
- Seashell Collector : A cozy iterative game about collecting seashells, befriending cute critters and contemplating the meaning of life.
